Implemented mechanics

- Repel coverage takes priority over partner protection and prevents consumption with 100% certainty.
- Every Repel-covered hatch creates a persistent popup and reports the remaining protection count.
- The fifth covered hatch reports that the Repel has worn off.
- Partner protection remains unchanged: without Repel coverage, a partner blocks 50% of actual attempts.
- Existing saves receive activeItemEffects.repelEggsRemaining = 0 without profile reset.
- Existing queued egg incident notices retain legacy predator information during migration.
